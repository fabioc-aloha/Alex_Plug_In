# Publication Targets for Alex Cognitive Architecture Paper

> Strategic analysis of journals, conferences, and trade publications for maximum impact

---

## Executive Summary

The Alex Cognitive Architecture paper sits at the intersection of **cognitive science**, **software engineering**, and **human-computer interaction**. This unique positioning opens multiple publication avenues, each with different audiences and impact profiles.

**Recommended Primary Target**: IEEE Software or Communications of the ACM
**Recommended Secondary Target**: CHI Conference (ACM)
**Recommended Trade Publication**: ACM Queue or InfoQ

## Recommended Submission Order

### Primary Path (Maximum Impact)

1. **InfoQ Article** (Week 1-2)
   - Build awareness, gather feedback
   - 2-3 week turnaround

2. **ACM Queue** (Month 1-2)
   - Practitioner credibility
   - Potential CACM republication

3. **CHI 2027** (September 2026 deadline)
   - Premier HCI venue
   - Add user study before submission

4. **IEEE Software** (Post-CHI)
   - Incorporate reviewer feedback
   - Journal archival version

### Alternative Path (Faster Academic Publication)

1. **IEEE Intelligent Systems** (Month 1)
   - Faster review cycle
   - Good visibility

2. **IUI 2027** (October 2026)
   - More focused community
   - Higher acceptance rate than CHI

3. **Cognitive Systems Research** (Post-conference)
   - Archival with cognitive science focus
