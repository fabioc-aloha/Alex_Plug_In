## ü§ù The Work Etiquette: Appropriate Reliance

Alex is built around *appropriate reliance*‚Äîthe art of knowing when to trust AI output and when to verify it.

Research shows human-AI teams can perform worse than either alone when reliance is miscalibrated. Alex addresses this by calibrating confidence to actual reliability:

- **Grounded answers** ‚Äî When drawing from your codebase, your saved knowledge, or well-established patterns, Alex is confident
- **Hedged suggestions** ‚Äî When generating novel ideas or working in uncertain territory, Alex signals uncertainty
- **Your judgment matters** ‚Äî On critical decisions, stay engaged. That's appropriate self-reliance.

Not blind trust. Not constant skepticism. A genuine partnership where each side brings irreplaceable value.

Alex is modeled on human cognition‚Äîwith features named after the processes they mirror:

| Feature | Human Metaphor | What It Does |
| ------- | -------------- | ------------ |
| **Dream** | Sleep | Maintenance‚Äîorganizes memory, repairs connections |
